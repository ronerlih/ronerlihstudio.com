HOLD ME
At my studio and Bedlam Lorenz Assmbly , fl.

Link to the show

Hold me is a 3d scan of myself sitting in my studio, naked with a phone. It was exhibited as part of a show in the F.A.T art district in Florida which I couldn't attend in person. It turned out to be an experiment in digital presence.
