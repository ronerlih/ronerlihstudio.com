inVisiBle

Opens at the Young at art museum Florida
February 15th through september 2017


—

inVisiBle is a mobile AR game, at the museum or any where else.
Find all 3 images, a recycling sign, a smiley face, and a broken heart
to unlock the online museum!!

Question and answers

  • What is the online Museum?
      • the Young at art online museum is an online extention of the Young at art museum in Florida. It shows a changing digital exhibtion ontop of the museum logo,
      and yes! you are very welcome to submit.
      It is a digital addition, like any other Augmented reality. Just like the other 'invisible' artworks that appear on the other signs, the online-museum appears on the Young At Art Museum logo:

    The Young at art museum card
    (youTube logo stands for the layered-augmented digital addition)
  • So, the game?
      • Yes, find the 3 signs (..and the secret lever ..hash hash), unlock the museum and see the changing exhibition at the online museum, enjoy, share your photos, have a ball
  • What kind of artwork can be submittd?
      • We are welcoming all submissions of digital artworks Here , artworks are a file you can send us or a link. It can be images - also photos of work, audio, video, GIFs, text, websites or you tell us. Please write us with any question.
